Music in my Life

Piano Competitions

To many young pianists competitions are everything. Before one becomes famous and has steady appearances at a concert hall, their greatest venue is in competitions. The Chopin Competition, in Poland, and the Van Cliburn Competition, in Texas, are two of the most famous piano competitions for budding pianists.

The Waring International Piano Competition in Palm Desert, CA

This competition was the very best competition I have ever been to. With a wonderful location, kind staff, and large prizes, it was amazing. I was awarded second prize. I am classifying my pieces into different categories, each of which will be indicated by a color. Etudes, or more technical pieces, will be in red; emotional pieces will be in purple, modern pieces will be in blue, and other pieces will be in green.
